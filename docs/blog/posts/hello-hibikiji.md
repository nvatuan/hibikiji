# Hello, Hibikiji

I have always wanted a personal blog. After a long while of pondering over what
to use, I got tired of the indecision and just picked something and went with it.

<!-- more -->

## Why "Hibikiji"

As a self-acclaimed weeaboo, I wanted to use the word `kiji` (記事 — *article*,
*writing*) but I also like the character **Hibiki**, so I mashed them together.
Tasteless? Absolutely. Acknowledging it doesn't make it less so.

This blog is mostly for me to look back on later — technology notes, places I've
been, cooking experiments, and stray thoughts. Writing keeps me sane. Hopefully I
don't get tired of it.
