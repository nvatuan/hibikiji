# Three days in Kyoto

A short trip that turned into my favorite kind of travel: no fixed plan, just
walking until something interesting shows up.

<!-- more -->

Loose highlights:

- Early mornings at the quieter temples before the crowds arrive.
- Getting deliberately lost in the backstreets of Gion.
- Coffee in a machiya that had been converted into a tiny café.
